Document Checklist

What You Need to Provide.

To prepare and file your business registration accurately, please gather the following information before we begin. Having these details ready ensures a smooth, efficient process — no delays, no back-and-forth.

Information to Provide

  • Legal Business Name Preferences

    Provide 1–3 name choices in order of preference. Your first available choice will be filed.

  • Entity Type

    Sole proprietor, LLC, corporation, partnership, or nonprofit — let us know which structure fits your goals.

  • Owner or Officer Details

    Full legal name(s), title(s), and contact information for all owners, members, or officers.

  • Principal Business Address

    The physical street address where your business operates in Washington State (P.O. Boxes not accepted for principal address).

  • Registered Agent Details

    Name and Washington State address of your registered agent, if you have one designated. We can advise if you need assistance.

  • Existing State Reference Numbers

    If updating or amending an existing filing, provide your UBI number or Secretary of State filing ID.

Helpful Context

Name Availability

Washington State requires that business names be distinguishable from existing registrations. We conduct a preliminary name check as part of our process — no extra steps needed from you.

Choosing the Right Entity

Not sure whether to register a business name as a sole proprietor or form an LLC? Each structure carries different liability and tax implications. We're happy to walk you through the differences before you decide.

Registered Agent Requirement

LLCs and corporations in Washington State must maintain a registered agent with a physical in-state address. If you don't have one, we can point you toward appropriate options.

Amendments & Updates

Changing your business address, adding a member, or updating officer information? Amendments are filed separately. Your UBI number from the original filing is all we need to get started.

Secure Handling

All personal and business information you share with us is handled with strict confidentiality. We never share your details with third parties outside of required state filings.
